Privacy Policy

View Policy

At Al Majal Business Park, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our facilities and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Personal identification information (name, date of birth, nationality)
  • Contact information (email address, phone number, residential address)
  • Employment details and company affiliation
  • Residency documentation and proof of address
  • Facility booking and usage history

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Verify residency status and eligibility for facility access
  • Process and manage facility bookings
  • Communicate important updates and announcements
  • Ensure security and safety within the business park
  • Improve our services and user experience

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Our security protocols include encrypted data storage, secure access controls, and regular security audits.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or government authorities
  • With your explicit consent
  • With service providers who assist in our operations
  • In emergency situations to protect safety and security

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request corrections to your data
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • File a complaint about data handling

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze usage patterns. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

7. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes through appropriate channels.

For questions about our Privacy Policy or to exercise your privacy rights, please contact our Privacy Officer at privacy@almajal.com
